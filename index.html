About us


Since October 19, 2001, PJSC "AVTOKAPITAL" is the official distributor of Mercedes-Benz Group AG in Ukraine, which is part of UKRAVTO GROUP.

The main areas of the company's activity are the sale of new cars, commercial and cargo segments, cars with mileage, development of after-sales service and dealer network. As of October 2021, the dealer network of PJSC "AVTOKAPITAL" has 21 car sales and service offices in the largest cities of Ukraine. Since September 2017, a specialized sales center for Mercedes-AMG cars (AMG Performance Center) has been operating in the capital dealership "Automobilny Dim Ukraine "Mercedes-Benz".
